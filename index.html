Our Story

Preserving the art of traditional perfume making since 1985

Heritage in Every Drop

Established in 1985, Ittar Ale has been the trusted name for traditional perfumes in the region. Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, started this business with a vision to preserve the ancient art of itra making.

We source the finest natural ingredients from across India and follow traditional methods to create fragrances that evoke nostalgia and elegance. Each itra is crafted with care, ensuring the highest quality and authenticity.
